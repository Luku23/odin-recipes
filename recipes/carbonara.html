Pasta Carbonara

Description

Carbonara is a beloved Italian pasta dish known for its rich, creamy texture and savory depth. The pasta, typically spaghetti or rigatoni, is coated in a luxurious sauce made from eggs and Pecorino Romano cheese, giving it a silky, golden appearance. Scattered throughout are crispy, caramelized pieces of guanciale or pancetta, adding a smoky, salty crunch to each bite. The dish is finished with a generous sprinkle of freshly ground black pepper, which enhances the flavors with its subtle heat. The final presentation is simple yet elegant, with the pasta glistening in its creamy coating, inviting you to dive into its comforting warmth.

Ingredients:

  • Spagetti
  • Chesse of your liking
  • An egg
  • Pepper
  • Bacon

Steps:

  1. Crack an egg, shread some cheese, put some pepper and mix it up
  2. Heat up a pan and brown some bacon
  3. Cook spagetti to your liking
  4. Drain spagetti, put them in the plate
  5. Put the mixture in the plate, mix it up
  6. Shread some cheese on top and put some pepper
  7. Serve!
