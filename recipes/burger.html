Delicious Burger

Description

An empty burger sits on a plain white plate, with no patty, condiments, or toppings in sight. The bun is lightly toasted, with a golden-brown exterior that hints at warmth and crispiness. The top bun is slightly askew, revealing the untouched, fluffy interior of the bottom bun. The absence of any fillings emphasizes the simplicity of the bread, with no drips of sauce or edges of lettuce peeking out. It’s a minimalist representation of what could have been a hearty meal, leaving a sense of expectation and curiosity about what’s missing.

Ingredients:

  • Top Bun
  • Bottom Bun
  • Middle Bun (not necessary)

Steps:

  1. Buy some buns of your liking from a store
  2. Heat up the oven to 200 degrees celcius
  3. Take your Top and Bottom buns, put them in the oven with their insides facing down
  4. Heat them up to your liking
  5. Carefully take out the bottom bun, put it on a plate
  6. Carefully take out the top bun, put it on top of the bottom bun
  7. Serve!
